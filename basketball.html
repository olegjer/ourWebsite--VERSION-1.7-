Basketball

While it’s not as popular in the UK as it is in places like the USA, basketball is a sport already loved by many and is growing in popularity. Basketball is a full body workout, considered moderate-to-vigorous exercise. All that’s required to play is a ball and a hoop, equipment that can be found at most leisure/fitness centres and schools. It’s estimated that 1 hour of basketball burns around 630-750 calories, so with a standard game lasting just shy of that at 48 minutes, a game of basketball can burn around 500-600 calories. As a semi-contact sport played in short bursts, it builds both muscle and endurance in its players, improving their cardiovascular functions, as well as improving balance and coordination. Like other team-sports it of course promotes teamwork and communication skills between teammates, whilst also developing individual player’s self-discipline and concentration. If you’re looking for a fun way to get fit, while having fun and working as a team, choosing to play basketball is a slam dunk!
